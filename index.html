Zack Wilkins

Zack Wilkins is a college student at the University of Utah in Salt Lake City

Originaly From Seattle, Zack fell in love with the outdoors at an early age competing in cross country races from as early as elementary school. As he got older the races kept getting further and further. He ran his first half marathon in high school, and first marathon his freshman year of college in 3 hours 30 minutes. For some reason he decided to run further, and competed in the DC Peaks 55k in October 2025.

Zack is partly in Utah because he loves skiing, but as any Wasatch skier knows it can snow a foot in one resort, but barely a trace at another. He created a snowfall tracker to help navigate that.
